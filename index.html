Full & partial rewires

Period properties, new builds, extensions and everything in between — planned around the way you actually live, with minimal disruption and a full EICR on completion.

  • Landlord & homebuyer reports
02

Lighting design

Layered schemes that flatter architecture — feature pendants, LED strip, downlight calculation, and scene control. We work with interior designers or lead the spec ourselves.

  • Smart & scene-controlled lighting
  • Bathroom IP-rated schemes
  • Under-cabinet & feature wall
03

Fault finding & diagnostics

Tripping circuit breakers, flickering lights, mystery shocks, dead sockets — we trace the cause methodically with proper test equipment, then present clear options to fix it. Most callouts solved same-day.

  • RCD & MCB tripping diagnosis
  • Insulation resistance & earth fault testing
  • Dead circuit & broken neutral tracing
  • Burning smell & overheating investigation
  • Emergency callouts
04

Kitchen & bathroom fit-out

We coordinate with your kitchen fitter, tiler and designer so the first fix lands on time and the sockets land exactly where they should — not where the cable decided.

  • Island sockets & USB-C
  • IP44/IP65 zoned bathroom lighting
  • Heated towel rails & mirror circuits

    We walk the property, listen carefully, and agree on what's in, what's out, and what's possible. No pressure, no upsell.

    Plain-English pricing with labour, materials and timeline broken out clearly. What you sign is what you pay.
