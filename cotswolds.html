01

Listed-building literate

We work alongside heritage architects and conservation officers — and we know when not to start work without their sign-off. Listed building consent paperwork done right the first time.

02

Sympathetic to stone

Cotswold limestone resists the chase. We plan cable routes around joinery, behind skirting, through floor voids — keeping the original fabric of the building intact.

03

Holiday let & AirBnB ready

EICRs, PAT testing, smart locks, smoke and CO interlinks, EV charging for guests — the full landlord paperwork pack delivered within 48 hours of testing.

04

Quiet on site

Many of our jobs are second homes occupied for weekends only. We coordinate with housekeepers, work to your visit schedule, and lock up exactly the way we found it.
